Of all the rivers of the Indian subcontinent, the Ganga is perhaps the most beautiful. It originates from Gaumukh in the Garhwal region of the Himalayas and is a superb river for inflatable rafts. Rafting on its waters is an unusual experience and one to cherish. To ride these rapids and to come to terms with their strength and wildness is to grow in spirit and to nurture the very stuff that dreams are made of.
Our team & adventure consultants have more than 100 years of combined experience in the industry. folks are friendly, knowledgeable and quick to respond to your inquiries.
For those of you who have little time but want to gain the maximum in a short period, we offer special Day Rafting From Shivpuri to Rishikesh NIM Beach which are a mix of both rafting water sports giving you the best of all that the Himalayas have to offer. All the 16 excellent rapids that fall between Marine Drive and Rishikesh NIM Beach have been categorized into different classes or grades according to the international grading system. Rapids at Marine Drive are Grade III . These rapids are termed as 'difficult', as they feature serious obstacles to rafters sailing along. Irregular waves, rocks and many highs await you along this white water stretch of River Ganges. The passage is clear but narrow bringing challenges for adventurers while they navigate the Ganga river rapids.

Age Group & Ability

To be able to enjoy white water rafting along Grade III and Grade IV rapids at Kaudiyala, you need to fall into the age group of 20 years to 35 years. Adventure lovers should at least have a previous rafting experience of 3-4 times. It is strongly advisable not to run these white water rapids without prior experience and expertise. The boat operator must be a swimmer and adept at all the essential techniques of river rafting. Being physically fit and sound is a must to enjoy these Ganga rapids.

Arrive Rishikesh rafting office & giving you some safety instruction then drive to Rafting Point. Later, start from start point to Rishikesh. Today, we hit some of the most exciting rapidson the Ganga. Black Money, Three Blind Mice, Body Surfing, Cross Fire, return to Sender, Roller Coaster, Tea off, Golf Course, Club House, Initiation, Double Trouble, and Hilton and Terminator. One can try Body Surfing on this Section. Enjoying the riverbank life along the Ganga Arrive Rishikesh. Tour terminates
