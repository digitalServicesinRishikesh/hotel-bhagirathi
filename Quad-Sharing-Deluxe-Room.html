Quad Sharing Deluxe Room
( Offers quality hospitality and unrivaled facilities )
₹ 5,000 / Night 2 Pax Book Now


Our Quad Sharing Deluxe Room, a Two king bed, features all a guest needs during his stay: dedicated workspace with high-speed internet access, spacious washrooms with shower.

Designed for families and groups of friends traveling together, our Quad Sharing Deluxe Rooms offer the perfect blend of space, comfort, and togetherness. We understand that the best travel memories are made when shared, which is why these rooms are thoughtfully arranged to accommodate four adults without compromising on style or relaxation.

Room Layout & Amenities

  •      Spacious Accommodations: Featuring multiple high-quality bedding configurations (two King-size beds or customized arrangements) to ensure everyone enjoys a restful night.
  •      Generous Living Space: Extra floor area designed to handle group luggage comfortably while maintaining a clean, airy feel.
  •      Group Entertainment: A large LED TV and high-speed Wi-Fi so the whole group can stay connected and entertained.
  •      Modern En-suite Bathroom: Equipped with 24/7 hot water and premium amenities to accommodate the needs of multiple guests efficiently.
  •      Scenic Windows: Large windows that offer a shared view of the surrounding hills, filling the room with natural Himalayan light.

    • Ideal For:
  •      Families: A safe and cozy environment for parents and children to stay in one room.
  •      Yoga Groups: Convenient and cost-effective for practitioners visiting Rishikesh together.
  •     Adventure Seekers: The perfect "base camp" for friends planning treks or jungle safaris in Rajaji National Park.
